Süper Lig 2015-2016 Season Analysis: Key Findings
1. Goal Statistics
Total Goals Scored:

Fenerbahçe and Beşiktaş were the teams with the most home victories.

Beşiktaş and Fenerbahçe were also the most successful away teams.
The least successful away teams were Mersin İdman Yurdu and Eskişehirspor.

Beşiktaş achieved the most wins overall (24).
Fenerbahçe also performed strongly with a total of 23 wins.
The teams with the fewest wins were Mersin İdman Yurdu and Eskişehirspor.

The week with the most goals scored was week 11, with a total of 33 goals.
The week with the fewest goals scored was week 2, with a total of 16 goals.
4. Weekly Team Rankings
Fenerbahçe and Beşiktaş consistently ranked at the top throughout the season.
Mersin İdman Yurdu and Eskişehirspor consistently ranked at the bottom.
5. Average Goals per Match
The average number of goals per match throughout the season was 2.76, indicating a generally high-scoring league.
General Evaluation
Successful Teams:

Fenerbahçe and Beşiktaş were among the most successful teams in terms of both goals scored and goals conceded.
These two teams consistently performed well both at home and away, dominating the season.
Weaker Teams:

Mersin İdman Yurdu and Eskişehirspor were the weakest teams in terms of both goals scored and goals conceded.
These two teams struggled throughout the season, showing inconsistent performance.
Average Goals per Match:

An average of 2.76 goals per match indicates that the Süper Lig provided exciting and high-scoring matches for the fans.
These analyses provide valuable insights into the dynamics of the Süper Lig 2015-2016 season and help evaluate team performances comprehensively.
